The Square Roots

A square root of a number is a number such that when the number is divided by the square root equals the square root.

n ÷ Sqrt(n) = Sqrt(n) OR Sqrt(n) = n½

Since this is the opposite of squares, the logic is similar. If the input is the area of a square, the output is the length of the square's side.
